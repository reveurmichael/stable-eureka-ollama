The reward reflection indicates that the player has successfully completed a certain level of gameplay by reaching the target achievement or state goal.
This reflection can be useful for various reasons such as:
1. Feedback on progress and achievements: This reflection can provide valuable feedback to the player regarding their progress and achievements in completing the game. This feedback can help the player understand what they have achieved, how far away from their current goals they are, and any other relevant information that can be useful for the player in enhancing their overall gameplay experience.
2. Identification of areas needing improvement or optimization: This reflection can identify areas where the player may need to improve or optimize their gameplay experience by providing feedback on progress and achievements, identification of areas needing improvement or optimization, and any other relevant information that can be useful for the player in enhancing their overall gameplay experience.
